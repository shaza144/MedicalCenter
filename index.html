Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most common questions you may have about our services, to help you better understand your experience with us.

How can I book an appointment at the center?

You can easily book an appointment through our website or by calling customer service. We also provide the possibility of direct booking when visiting the center.

Do I need a medical referral to book an appointment with a specialist?

Not necessarily. You can book directly with the specialist you need without the need for a medical referral, depending on your condition.

Is health insurance accepted?

Yes, we deal with a wide range of health insurance companies. Please check the list of companies contracted with us to ensure your insurance covers you.

What are the center's working hours?

We work from 8 am to 10 pm seven days a week, to ensure that your health needs are met in a timely manner.

How can I know the results of medical tests?

The results of the tests are available through our electronic application or by visiting the center. We guarantee complete confidentiality and speedy delivery of the results.
